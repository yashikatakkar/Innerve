About Us



Innerve is the annual technical fest of Indira Gandhi Delhi Technical University for Women (IGDTUW), catering to the interests of numerous students across all fields. Experiencing a footfall of over 9000 students from all over the country every year, Innerve is a platform where technical acumen blends with creativity and innovation. A plethora of events is conducted as part of Innerve, including workshops, guest lectures, and events such as hackathons and contests where students can portray their technical prowess. Innerve launched its first online edition in 2020.
